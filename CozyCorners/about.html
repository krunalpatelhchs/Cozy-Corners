What is Cozy Corners?

Cozy Corners is a fine establishment that teaches young men and women life cooking skills; something that is super vital in the adult world. While at the same time it is teaching students a good work ethic and an appreciation for food preparation. Cozy Corners has done more than establish it's place in HCHS's history books, through all the communtiy work and delicious food they provide to the staff and student body. Normally they do all of their teaching on Monday, while Tuesday is generally their preperation day. On Wednesdays they usually feed the teachers that have put in food requests, normally a form will be provided beforehand with all of the food options.
