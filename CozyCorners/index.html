Welcome to Cozy Corners

Cozy Corners is a student-led restaurant that is overseen by Mrs. Joni Shank. Mrs. Cochran is the founder of Cozy Corners. They offer meals to the student body and faculty along with the community. In the past, they have made food for professional bands. Most meals range from $5 - $12.
